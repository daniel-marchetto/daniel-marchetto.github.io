Research

Research Interests

As an undergraduate, I had a great deal of interest in neuro-oncology and neuropsychology. I had special interest in information processing and executive attention. (I still find hemispatial neglect and anosognosia the most fascinating defects.) As a masters student, my interests also expanded to individual behavior around climate change. Now that I am going into my 3rd year in my doctoral training, my research interests revolve around individual and institutional behaviors relating to graduate students training for the workforce. I have strong methodological interests in measurement, data acquisition, and data analysis.
