Daniel J. Marchetto

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Public Policy, my M.S. in Statistics, and my M.S. in Computational Science and Engineering at Georgia Tech with the expectation of finishing my doctorate by 2023. I received my Masters in Public Policy from Arizona State and my B.S. in Psychology (Cognitive Neuroscience minor) from Carnegie Mellon.

Research

Research Interests

As an undergraduate, I mostly interested in neuro-oncology and neuropsychology. I had special interest in information processing and executive attention. (Hemispatial neglect and anosognosia are incredibly fascinating attentional deffects.) As a masters student, my interests also expanded to individual behavior around climate change. Now that I am in my 3rd year in my doctoral training, my research interests revolve around individual and institutional behaviors relating to the STEM workforce. I have strong methodological interests in measurement, data acquisition, and data analysis.
